>
> - If by any chance you can’t manage dinner tonight, perhaps we can at least have a drink together. （万一你今晚不能来吃饭，或许我们至少可以一起喝一杯。）
>

> [!WARNING]
>
> - whether, provided （（表条件）如果；即使；是否；（表假设）假如）
