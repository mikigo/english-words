## 2000. instead

**代替，顶替；反而**

> [!TIP]
>
> - instead of （代替；不是…而是…）
>

> [!NOTE]
>

>
> - Could I have tuna instead of ham? （我可否要金枪鱼来代替火腿？）
>
> - Instead of being annoyed, he seemed quite pleased. （他没有生气，反而似乎很高兴。）
